Tottington Public Band.

Welcome to the Website for the Tottington Public Band.

Welcome to the Web-site of one of, if not the, longest continuously performing brass band in the Country - Tottington Public Band from the village of Tottington, Lancashire. United Kingdom.

We hope that you enjoy the site. It has been designed to show the human, as well as the professional side of the band.

All band members are volunteers and play because they enjoy the music and camaraderie that comes from performing for you and competing.

If you have any comments about the band or the site, please contact us on the numbers of the contacts page or by email at webmaster@tottingtonpublicband.co.uk

We hope that you enjoy the site and that you will come and see us play, or better still, book us to play for you. All the information that you need is contained in this site.

We are proud to be part of the fine tradition of Lancashire for producing quality Brass Bands, and are available for all types of functions, please see our bookings page.
