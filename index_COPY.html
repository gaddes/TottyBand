Welcome to our website!

Tottington Public Band - from the village of Tottington, Lancashire - is the longest continuously performing brass band in the UK!

This site has been designed to show both the human and the professional side of the band. All band members are volunteers; they play for the love of music and the camaraderie built through public performances and competitions.

We hope that you will come and see us play or, better still, book us to play for you. To get in touch, please use the links on our contact page.

Alternatively, you can email us at webmaster@tottingtonpublicband.co.uk

We are proud to be part of Lancashire's fine tradition of producing quality Brass Bands. We love to perform, and we are available for all types of functions - please see our bookings page.
